General Guidance


From the Hong Kong International Airport to HKU

  • By Taxi
    • A direct taxi ride to the HKU main campus would take about 40 mins at a cost of approximately HKD400 (Via Western Harbour Tunnel). Note that taxis in HK have different colors with different operating islands, and in order to come to HKU campus, one should pick the line for "Hong Kong island" and take a RED taxi. Moreover, most taxis in HK only take HKD cash. One can take Uber if cash is not available.
  • By Airport Express
    • Take the Airport Express from the Airport to Hong Kong Station (one-way fare HKD115). On exiting the train, on the same floor, there are multiple taxi stands. A taxi ride to the HKU main campus would take about 20 mins at a cost of approximately HKD 80. Participants and guests may also walk to Central Station (about 10 mins) from Hong Kong Station, and take the MTR Island Line to HKU station. Please refer to the following links for details: Airport Express, MTR route map and fares, guide for using "Alipay" or "Octopus Card" to take MTR.


From the Hong Kong West Kowloon Station to HKU

  • By Taxi
    • Take taxi at taxi stand on B2 level. A direct taxi ride to the HKU main campus would take about 20 mins at a cost of approximately HKD200 (Via Western Harbour Tunnel). Note that taxis in HK have different colors with different operating islands, and in order to come to HKU campus, one should pick the line for "Hong Kong island" and take a RED taxi. Moreover, most taxis in HK only take HKD cash. One can take Uber if cash is not available.
  • By MTR
    • Transit to MTR Kowloon Station via the overhead bridge on Exit M and take the Tung Chung Line to Hong Kong Station. On exiting the train, take the escalator to L2 Floor, there are multiple taxi stands. A taxi ride to the HKU main campus would take about 20 mins at a cost of approximately HKD60. Participants and guests may also walk to Central Station (about 10 mins) from Hong Kong Station, and take the MTR Island Line to HKU station. Please refer to the following links for details: High Speed Rail, MTR route map and fares, guide for using "Alipay" or "Octopus Card" to take MTR.
